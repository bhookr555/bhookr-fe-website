## Image Specifications:
- **Before/After Photos**: 600x800px (portrait orientation)
- **Avatar Photos**: 200x200px (square)
- **Format**: JPG, PNG, or WebP
- Same angle/pose for before and after photos

## Image Guidelines:
- High quality, well-lit photos
- Same background/lighting for before and after (if possible)
- Appropriate and professional
- Client consent obtained for public display
- JPG format recommended

## Privacy:
- Ensure you have written consent from clients before displaying transformation photos
- Consider privacy laws and regulations
- You can use stock photos for demo purposes initially
